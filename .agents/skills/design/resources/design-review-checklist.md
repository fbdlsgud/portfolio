# Design Review Checklist

- Primary user task is visible and reachable.
- Navigation and information hierarchy match the app domain.
- Loading, empty, error, disabled, and success states are specified.
- Labels and actions use concrete product language.
- Touch targets, keyboard access, contrast, and focus states are considered.
- Responsive behavior is defined for the project's target devices.
- The design reuses existing components and tokens when they exist.
