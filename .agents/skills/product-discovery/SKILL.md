# Product Discovery Skill

Use when the user gives an app idea, rough feature list, target stack, or MVP
direction.

## Process

1. Identify app type and target users.
2. Separate confirmed requirements from suggestions.
3. Suggest missing MVP features.
4. Suggest later-phase features.
5. Surface open questions.
6. Update `docs/ai/conventions.md`.
7. Avoid treating suggestions as confirmed until approved.
